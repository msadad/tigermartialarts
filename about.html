About Us

About Tiger Martial Arts

At Tiger Martial Arts, we are committed to empowering individuals through the discipline and strength of martial arts. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced fighter, our programs are tailored to suit all ages and skill levels.

With expert instructors, modern training facilities, and a supportive community, we offer a range of classes including Karate, Kickboxing, Fitness Training, and Self Defence. Our goal is to build confidence, improve fitness, and inspire personal growth in a safe and inclusive environment.

Our Journey

Founded by passionate martial arts professionals, Tiger Martial Arts started with a vision to spread the timeless values of discipline, respect, and inner strength. What began as a small community dojo has grown into one of Dubai’s leading martial arts academies — trusted by families, schools, and individuals alike.

Our Programs

  • Karate: Traditional martial arts training focused on precision, power, and self-discipline.
  • Judo: A grappling-based martial art that teaches control, balance, and effective throwing techniques.
  • Jiu-jitsu: Ground-based combat training that develops submission techniques, control, and flexibility.
  • Muay Thai: A powerful striking art using fists, elbows, knees, and shins—perfect for endurance and self-defence.
  • Self Defence: Practical techniques for real-world safety situations, tailored for all ages and experience levels.
  • Kids Classes: Engaging and structured sessions to build confidence, respect, and focus in young learners.
  • Open Mat: Unstructured training time for practice, sparring, or refining techniques with peers.

Our Mission

We aim to inspire people of all backgrounds to unlock their full potential through martial arts. Our mission is not just to teach fighting techniques but to instill confidence, resilience, leadership, and respect — both on and off the mat.

State-of-the-Art Facilities

Our training center in Dubai is equipped with professional-grade flooring, safety gear, strength equipment, and air-conditioned halls. Every detail is designed to create a safe, comfortable, and motivating environment.
